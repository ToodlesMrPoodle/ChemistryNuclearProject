Smoke Detectors

Should they be used?

By Laly and Tou

Background Information

Smoke detectors were made accidentally by a Swiss physicist working on a way to detect poison gas. His device failed and Walter Jaeger in frustration lit a cigarette, the smoke moved the meter on his device. His accidental discovery led to the commercial use of the modern smoke detector that we all use today in our homes.

After many years of the invention being passed around by many scientists and inventors, the first “modern” smoke detector came to be. Duane Pearsall along with Stanley Bennett Peterson created the first “modern” smoke detector that was battery powered in the mid 20th century.
